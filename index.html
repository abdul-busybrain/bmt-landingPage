    How it works

    Consultation in 4 simple steps

    01

    Create an account

    To get started, the first step is to create your account. You'll begin by signing up using your email address, Verify your email. Then log in to acess your dashboard.

    02

    Update your profile

    Add details such as your country and occupation to personalize your account. You can also specify the services you offer and set your availability by indicating the days and time ranges you are available for consultations.

    03

    Share your public profile

    Once your profile is updated, share your public profile link with potential clients. This allows them to view your services and availability, making it easy for them to book consultations with you.

    04

    Do your meetings and earn.

    Conduct your scheduled consultations and earn for each session you complete. Our platform ensures a seamless experience for both you and your clients.
